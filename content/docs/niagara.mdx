This section focuses on Niagara assets and their internals.

## 5.1 No Spaces, Ever

As mentioned in the [Forbidden Characters](/docs/principles#001-forbidden-characters) section, spaces and all white space characters are forbidden in identifiers. This is especially true for Niagara systems as it makes working with things significantly harder — if not impossible — when working with HLSL or other means of scripting within Niagara and trying to reference an identifier.
